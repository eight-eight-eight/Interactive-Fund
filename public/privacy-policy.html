4. Data Security

We employ Military-Grade AES-256 Encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. Our servers are housed in Tier 4 data centers with 24/7 physical surveillance and redundant security protocols.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only share information with trusted third-party service providers (such as cloud hosting and KYC specialists) who are contractually bound by our strict privacy standards.

6. Financial Data Protection

Wallet details and transaction histories are isolated from primary identity databases. We use tokenization to ensure your actual financial credentials never enter our main processing environment.

7. KYC & Compliance
